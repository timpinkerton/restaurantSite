Welcome to Egg City!

Egg City Diner is all about the egg. We serve up all kinds of eggs, at all times of the day... and at all times of the night. We're open 24/7.

We specialize in local, organic, free range, super delicious, freshest of the fresh eggs. All of our eggs are produced locally from our own Egg City Farm located just 8 feet from our kitchen. It doesn't get more local than that! You can view our egg producing operation right from your table... while you're eating eggs. We guarantee that any egg you eat was layed less than 4 hours ago.
